About Me

Hi! My name is Joshua Lytle. I am fascinated with science and scientific computing technology - and got to explore each of these while working on my PhD in mathematics (June 2017). My favorite tool to use as a data scientist is Python - although R has its own strengths. Python really shines as a general-purpose programming language, with its clear syntax and a wonderful, responsive community of users.

When I am not programming, looking up a new cool machine learning algorithm, or exploring a dataset, I can be found working on the classical guitar or spending time with my family.
